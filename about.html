I'm a graduate student at the Illinois Institute of Technology pursuing a dual degree in Computer Science (B.S.) and Data Science (M.A.S.), with an expected graduation in May 2027. Currently maintaining a 3.5 GPA while actively involved in campus organizations and athletics.

I believe in the intersection of technology and creativity, always looking for ways to merge analytical thinking with innovative solutions.
